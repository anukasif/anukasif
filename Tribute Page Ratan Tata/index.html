About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata, the former chairman of the Tata Group, is known for his humility, leadership, and vision. Under his guidance, Tata Group became one of the largest and most respected companies in India. His remarkable leadership, which combined ethics and innovation, transformed not only businesses but also the lives of many people.
